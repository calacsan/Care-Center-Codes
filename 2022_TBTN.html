The Care Center Invites You to Take Back the Night!

TAKE BACK THE NIGHT

with the Care Center

October is Domestic Violence Awareness Month.
The Care Center invites you to Take Back the Night event on October 25, 2022 from 4PM - 6PM at the SU Ballroom to raise awareness for the end of sexual violence and to support survivors in their healing journeys.

Take Back The Night is the oldest worldwide movement to stand against sexual violence in all forms. Events held bring communities together to empower survivors and remind them that they are not alone.

Communities from UNLV, CSN & NSC are welcomed to come and participate. We'll be holding space to allow guests to share their stories through an open mic, open to all forms of storytelling (speak-out, poetry, song, art piece, etc.).
